How to Pick a Brand or Domain Name

Simple advice for choosing a memorable, usable name.

Good names are easy to pronounce, easy to spell, and not overly restrictive if your project grows.

Before committing, check domain availability, social handles, and trademark risk.

Shortlists are more useful than huge lists. Pick three to five good candidates and test them out loud.
